### ategory ID Mapping and Descriptions:

1. **Data & Analytics** – Data processing, analysis, and visualization tools, including data pipelines, statistical analysis, and BI tools.
2. **Cloud Services** – Computing, storage, and virtualization infrastructure or platform services.
3. **Project & Collaboration Management** – Team collaboration, task management, project planning, and office automation tools.
4. **Code Hosting** – Code version control and collaborative development platforms (e.g., Git, GitHub, GitLab).
5. **Documentation & Knowledge Base** – Document management, knowledge management, wiki, writing, and note-taking tools.
6. **Multimedia** – Audio, video, and image editing or creation tools, including UI/UX design tools.
7. **Social Media** – Social networks, online communities, and content publishing or interaction platforms.
8. **Communication & Email** – Email, instant messaging, notifications, conferencing, or other communication tools.
9. **Development & Testing** – Programming, IDEs, development frameworks, testing tools, or container platforms.
10. **Command Execution Tools** – Command-line utilities, automation scripts, and operations tools.
11. **Security & Monitoring** – Cybersecurity, authentication, access control, logging, and monitoring systems.
12. **Blockchain & Finance** – Cryptocurrency, payment, financial, or e-commerce platforms.
13. **Geography & Transportation** – Maps, navigation, location services, transportation, and mobility tools.
14. **AI Tools** – Artificial intelligence tools, including machine learning, generative AI, and intelligent analytics.
15. **Information Retrieval** – Search engines, web scraping, and content retrieval tools.
16. **Others** – Applications that do not fit clearly into the above categories or cannot be determined.

### Rules:

1. Output **CSV only**, with no extra text, comments, or explanations.
2. If an application’s function is unclear or cannot be determined, classify it as **“Others” (Category 16)**.
3. Determine the category based on your knowledge of the application.
4. Keep the **output order the same as the input order**.
